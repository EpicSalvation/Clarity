Features

Everything you need to run worship presentations — designed for simplicity, built for reliability.

Slide Backgrounds

Make every slide visually compelling with flexible background options that range from simple solid colors to full-motion video.

Solid Colors & Gradients

Choose from any color with a full color picker, or create smooth linear gradients between two colors. Set the gradient angle to match your visual style. Great for a clean, modern look that keeps the focus on your lyrics.

Image Backgrounds

Use any image as a slide background. Images are automatically scaled to fill the output display. Combine with text shadow and outline settings to ensure your words stay readable over any photo.

Video Backgrounds

Add motion to your presentations with looping video backgrounds. Supports common formats like MP4 and WebM. Videos play seamlessly behind your text on the output display.

Cascading Backgrounds

Set a background once on a song or slide group, and it automatically applies to every slide within that group. No need to set the same background on each slide individually. Override any individual slide when you want something different.

Import

Bring existing content into Clarity. Import PowerPoint presentations or individual slide images to build your service quickly.

Import PowerPoint

Have slides in PowerPoint already? Import them directly with Ctrl+Shift+P. Clarity exports each slide as an image and adds them to your presentation automatically. Works with any .pptx file on Windows.

Import Slide Images

Import PNG, JPG, or BMP files as slide backgrounds with Ctrl+Shift+I. Select multiple images at once and they'll be added as a slide group in natural sort order. Perfect for importing slides from other presentation software or custom graphics.

Screenshot: Imported PowerPoint slides displayed as image backgrounds in the slide grid

Song Library

Build and manage your worship song collection. Import from popular formats, search instantly, and add songs to your service in seconds.

Import from SongSelect & OpenLyrics

Already have songs in CCLI SongSelect or OpenLyrics XML format? Import them directly. Clarity reads the lyrics, verse structure, and metadata so you don't have to retype anything.

Search & Filter

Find any song in your library by title, lyrics, or author. Results appear as you type. When your library grows to hundreds of songs, you'll still find what you need in under a second.

Verse & Section Structure

Songs are organized by verse, chorus, bridge, and other sections. Reorder sections per-service without changing the original song. Show only the verses you need for this week's arrangement.

CCLI Reporting

Track which songs you use and when. Clarity records CCLI numbers and usage data to help you stay compliant with your church's CCLI license reporting requirements.

Copyright Compliance

Display CCLI song numbers and your church's license number on song title slides automatically. At the end of your service, Clarity can generate a copyright slide that collects all song and scripture attribution notices in one place — keeping you compliant with licensing terms and API requirements. All copyright and CCLI options live on a dedicated settings page with quick access to the CCLI Usage Report.

Scripture Support

Three scripture sources in one unified dialog. Local Bible database for offline use, ESV API for the English Standard Version, and API.bible for access to 2,500+ Bible versions across 1,600+ languages.

Unified Scripture Dialog

All three scripture sources are available in a single tabbed dialog (Ctrl+B). Switch between Local Bible, ESV API, and API.bible without leaving the window. Each tab remembers its own search state and settings.

Local Bible Database

Scripture is stored locally on your computer. Look up any book, chapter, and verse without an internet connection. Import additional translations in OSIS, USFM, USX, USFX, Zefania, or TSV formats. Perfect for churches with unreliable Wi-Fi or off-site events.

ESV API

Access the English Standard Version directly from the ESV API. Just type a reference like "Romans 8:28" and the text is fetched instantly. Requires a free API key from the ESV website.

API.bible — 2,500+ Versions

Connect to API.bible for access to over 2,500 Bible versions across 1,600+ languages. Browse by language and version, search by reference, and preview passages before inserting. Requires a free API key from scripture.api.bible.

Red-Letter Edition

Words of Christ are displayed in red, just like a red-letter Bible. Supported by local imports and compatible API.bible versions. This visual distinction helps the congregation recognize when Jesus is speaking.

Smart Verse Splitting

Long passages are automatically split across multiple slides with readable amounts of text on each. Choose one verse per slide or combine them. Verse numbers are preserved so readers can follow along in their own Bibles.

Scripture Themes

Scripture slides can have their own dedicated theme, separate from your song slides. Set a background, font, and color scheme specifically for Bible readings that applies automatically.

Live Output

Send your presentation to any screen, projector, or streaming pipeline. Clarity's multi-process architecture means your output never drops, even if the control app has an issue.

Multi-Monitor Fullscreen

The output display runs as its own fullscreen window. Drag it to your projector or secondary monitor. Your control interface stays on your main screen where you manage the service.

Confidence Monitor

A second output designed for your speaker or worship leader. Shows the current slide, upcoming slide, and a service timer. Positioned on a stage monitor so the presenter always knows what's next.

NDI Streaming

Send your presentation output as an NDI video stream over your local network. Perfect for integrating with OBS, vMix, or any other production software for livestreaming and recording.

Blackout & Whiteout

Instantly blank the output display with a single click or keyboard shortcut. Choose between a black screen or white screen. The presentation stays in place — just toggle back when you're ready to show it again.

Process Isolation

The output display runs in its own process, separate from the control app. If anything goes wrong on the control side, the output keeps showing the current slide. Your congregation never sees a crash.

Remote Control

Control your presentation from anywhere in the room using your phone or tablet. No app to install — it's a web interface that works in any browser.

Web-Based Interface

Clarity runs a lightweight web server on your local network. Open the URL on any device with a browser — iPhone, Android, iPad, laptop — and you have full control. Nothing to download or install.

QR Code Connect

Display a QR code on the control screen that anyone can scan to connect instantly. No need to type URLs or remember IP addresses. Hand your phone to a volunteer and they're ready to go.

PIN Security

Protect your presentation with a 4-digit PIN. Only authorized devices can connect and control the output. Change the PIN anytime from the control app.

Slide Navigation

The remote shows your full slide list with visual previews. Tap to jump to any slide, or use the large next/previous buttons for simple linear navigation. Works great for worship leaders who just need forward and back.

Themes & Styling

Look professional without a design degree. Built-in themes handle typography, colors, and layout so your slides look great from the start.

15+ Built-in Themes

Choose from a curated set of themes designed for worship projection. Each theme includes a coordinated background, font pairing, and color scheme. Switch themes per-item or apply one to your entire service.

Text Legibility Controls

Add text outlines, drop shadows, and background overlays to ensure your words are always readable. These controls work automatically with any background — solid colors, images, or video.

Font & Size Customization

Choose from any font installed on your system. Adjust font size, weight, and alignment per slide or per theme. Large text for lyrics, smaller text for scripture references — you're in control.

Smooth Transitions

Slides transition with a clean fade effect. No jarring cuts between slides. The transition is fast enough to feel instant but smooth enough to look polished on the big screen.
